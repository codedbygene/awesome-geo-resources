# What is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the next evolution of SEO.

Instead of optimizing only for Google rankings, GEO focuses on helping AI systems:

* Understand your content
* Reference your website
* Recommend your products
* Surface your expertise

Popular AI search systems include:

* ChatGPT
* Claude
* Gemini
* Perplexity
* Microsoft Copilot
